# Korea Stock Market Context

## Market Structure

| Exchange | Full Name | Focus | Index |
|----------|-----------|-------|-------|
| KRX | Korea Exchange | Both markets | — |
| KOSPI | Korea Composite Stock Price Index | Large-cap | KOSPI Index |
| KOSDAQ | Korea Securities Dealers Automated Quotations | Small/mid-cap, tech | KOSDAQ Index |

**KOSPI** is the main benchmark (~800 large companies). **KOSDAQ** is where high-growth tech and biotech companies list — more volatile, Gen Z-favored.

## Korea Gen Z Investor Profile

- Heavy users of **Toss (토스)** — the super-app that normalized investing for young Koreans
- **Kakao Pay** and **Naver Financial** also significant
- Reddit equivalent: **Naver Café**, **Daum Café**, and **KakaoTalk open chats**
- Very tuned into global trends — US tech stocks (Tesla, NVIDIA, Apple) popular alongside domestic
- K-pop stock trading is emotionally driven — album releases, concerts, group news move stock price

## Korean Fintech Apps — Design Benchmarks

### Toss (토스)
- Gold standard for Gen Z Korean fintech UX
- Known for: radical simplicity, one-action-per-screen, soft rounded UI, progressive disclosure
- Color: White-dominant with blue (#0064FF) accent — clean, trustworthy but modern
- Features Gen Z love: instant transfers, spending insights, credit score, mini investment

### Kakao Pay
- More colorful — Kakao Yellow (#FEE500) with black text
- Playful, emoji-native, deeply integrated with KakaoTalk social graph
- Gamification elements: badges, streaks

### Naver Pay / Naver Financial
- More information-dense than Toss
- Navy + green palette
- Trusted by slightly older demographic
