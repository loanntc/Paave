# Vietnam Stock Market Context

## Market Structure

| Exchange | Full Name | Focus | Index |
|----------|-----------|-------|-------|
| HoSE | Ho Chi Minh Stock Exchange | Large-cap, blue chips | VN-Index |
| HNX | Hanoi Stock Exchange | Mid/small-cap, bonds | HNX-Index |
| UPCoM | Unlisted Public Company Market | Pre-listing companies | UPCoM Index |

**VN-Index** is the primary benchmark (~500 stocks). Think of it like Vietnam's S&P 500.

## Key Sectors Gen Z Investors Watch in Vietnam

- **Banking & Finance**: VCB (Vietcombank), TCB (Techcombank), VPB (VP Bank), MBB (MB Bank) — dominant, always liquid
- **Real Estate**: VHM (Vinhomes), NVL, DIG — volatile but exciting for younger traders
- **Technology & Telecom**: FPT (largest tech co), VGI
- **Consumer/Retail**: MWG (Mobile World), PNJ (jewelry)
- **Industrial & Manufacturing**: HPG (Hoa Phat Steel), GVR

## Vietnam Gen Z Investor Profile

- Age 22–27 mostly entering market since 2020–2021 boom
- Heavily influenced by YouTube finance channels (e.g., Topi, FiinGroup content)
- Facebook groups and Zalo groups are primary community channels (not Reddit)
- Common entry via SSI, VPS, VCSC brokerage apps
- F0 (first-time investor) culture is huge — many are still learning basics

## Reliable VN Market Sources

- **CafeF** (cafef.vn) — leading financial news in Vietnamese
- **VNDirect** (vndirect.com.vn) — broker research reports
- **SSI Research** — respected analyst reports
- **FiinGroup** (fiingroup.vn) — institutional data
- **VietStock** (vietstock.vn) — data and charts
- **Investing.com/VN** — international format

## Common Ticker Mistakes to Avoid

- HoSE and HNX use different suffixes in some data providers — always specify the exchange
- VCB ≠ VCG (two different companies)
- Some tickers look identical across exchanges — always double-check
