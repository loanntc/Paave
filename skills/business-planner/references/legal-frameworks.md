## Business Structure Comparison

| Structure | Liability | Tax Treatment | Best For | Complexity |
|-----------|-----------|---------------|----------|------------|
| Sole Proprietorship | Unlimited personal | Pass-through | Solo, low-risk | Very low |
| Partnership | Shared personal | Pass-through | 2+ founders, early stage | Low |
| LLC / Ltd | Limited | Flexible | Most small businesses | Medium |
| Corporation (C/S) | Limited | Corporate / Pass-through | Raising investment | High |
| Cooperative | Limited | Pass-through | Community-owned | Medium |

---

## Registration Process (General Steps)
1. Choose and verify business name availability
2. Register with national/local business registry
3. Obtain Tax ID / EIN (or equivalent)
4. Open a dedicated business bank account
5. Register for relevant taxes (VAT, GST, sales tax)
6. Apply for industry-specific licenses/permits

## Region-Specific Notes

### Vietnam 🇻🇳
- Common structures: Single-member LLC (Công ty TNHH MTV), Multi-member LLC, Joint Stock Company
- Register via: National Business Registration Portal (dangkykinhdoanh.gov.vn)
- Foreign ownership restrictions apply in certain sectors
- Required: Business registration certificate, tax code, seal registration
- Relevant law: Enterprise Law 2020, Investment Law 2020

### United States 🇺🇸
- LLC most common for small business
- Register in state of operation (or Delaware for investment)
- EIN from IRS.gov
- State-level business licenses vary

### European Union 🇪🇺
- GDPR compliance mandatory for any customer data
- Structure varies by country (GmbH, SAS, Ltd, etc.)
- VAT registration typically required above turnover threshold

### Southeast Asia (General)
- Most countries allow 100% foreign ownership in select sectors
- Free trade zones offer tax incentives
- Check ASEAN Investment Portal for country-specific rules

## Intellectual Property Checklist
- [ ] Trademark your business name and logo (national + international if needed)
- [ ] Patent novel products/processes (file before public disclosure)
- [ ] Register copyright for original creative works
- [ ] Use NDAs with employees, contractors, and partners
- [ ] Secure domain name and social handles early
- [ ] Draft clear IP ownership clauses in co-founder agreements

---

## Key Contracts to Have Early
1. **Co-founder agreement** — equity split, roles, vesting schedule, exit terms
2. **Employment / contractor agreements** — IP assignment, non-compete clauses
3. **Client service agreement** — scope, payment, liability limits
4. **Supplier/vendor contracts** — pricing, delivery, quality standards
5. **Privacy policy + Terms of Service** — mandatory for any digital product

---

## Tax Obligations Overview
- Corporate income tax (varies by country: Vietnam ~20%, US ~21%, Singapore ~17%)
- Value-added tax / GST registration threshold
- Payroll tax and social insurance for employees
- Withholding tax on payments to foreign contractors
- Transfer pricing rules for businesses with related parties

*Always engage a local accountant for actual filing obligations.*
