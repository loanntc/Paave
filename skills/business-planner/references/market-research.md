## Market Sizing Framework

### TAM / SAM / SOM

```
TAM (Total Addressable Market)
└── Everyone who could ever buy this
    └── SAM (Serviceable Addressable Market)
        └── The portion you can realistically reach
            └── SOM (Serviceable Obtainable Market)
                └── Realistic target in Year 1–3
```

**Top-Down Approach**: Start with industry report size → apply % segments
**Bottom-Up Approach**: Count potential customers × average spend = market size
*Use both and triangulate.*

## Primary Research Methods (Doing It Yourself)

### Customer Interviews (Best for validation)
- Target: 10–20 interviews with your ideal customer
- Goal: Understand pain, current behavior, willingness to pay
- Key questions:
  - "Walk me through the last time you faced [problem]"
  - "What do you use today to solve it?"
  - "What's most frustrating about current solutions?"
  - "What would you pay for a perfect solution?"
- Avoid: Leading questions, pitching your idea too early

### Surveys
- Use: Google Forms, Typeform, or social media polls
- Sample size: 50+ for directional insight, 200+ for confidence
- Best for: Quantifying what interviews reveal qualitatively

### Landing Page Test
- Build a simple page describing the product
- Drive traffic via ads or social
- Measure: email signups, waitlist joins, or mock purchases
- Cost: $100–$500 for a quick validation

### Competitor Customer Mining
- Read 1-star AND 5-star reviews on competitors (G2, Trustpilot, App Store, Amazon)
- 1-star = unmet needs = your opportunity
- 5-star = what customers value most = match or beat it

| Source | What You'll Find |
|--------|-----------------|

| Statista / IBISWorld | Market size, industry reports |

| App Store / Play Store reviews | Product feedback on competitors |

| Trade associations | Industry benchmarks and reports |

## Demand Validation Checklist
- [ ] Can you name 10 real people who would pay for this today?
- [ ] Have you interviewed at least 5 potential customers?
- [ ] Is there evidence people search for this problem online?
- [ ] Are there competitors making money in this space? (good sign)
- [ ] Has anyone pre-paid, signed up, or expressed strong intent?
- [ ] Do customers describe the problem in the same words unprompted?

## Market Trend Analysis
Look for signals across:
- **Macro trends**: Demographics, economic shifts, regulatory changes
- **Technology trends**: What new tools enable this business?
- **Cultural trends**: Changing values, behaviors, preferences
- **Competitive trends**: Are incumbents investing here or pulling back?

Tools: Google Trends, Exploding Topics, SparkToro, LinkedIn Insights
